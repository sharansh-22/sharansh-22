## Featured Work

<br>

**[Credit Risk Platform](https://github.com/sharansh-22/CRIS/tree/main/systems/credit_risk)**
Leakage-free credit risk modelling on 1.34M LendingClub loans. Systematic feature auditing, out-of-time validation, SHAP-based explainability, stealth defaulter analysis, and economic impact simulation.
<br>`LightGBM` · `SHAP` · `Pandas` · `Scikit-learn`

<br>

**[CRIS — Cascade Risk Intelligence System](https://github.com/sharansh-22/CRIS)**
A quantitative research program investigating whether macroeconomic signals improve financial decision systems. Key finding: borrower-centric models consistently outperformed direct macro integration — negative results were documented rather than discarded.
<br>`XGBoost` · `LightGBM` · `NumPy` · `Matplotlib`

<br>

**[Research-OS](https://github.com/sharansh-22/Research-OS)**
Research-oriented RAG system for ML literature. Hybrid retrieval (FAISS + BM25), DeBERTa NLI verification, hallucination auditing, math-aware document ingestion, and full research provenance tracking.
<br>`FastAPI` · `React` · `FAISS` · `DeBERTa`
